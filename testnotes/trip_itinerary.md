# TRAVEL LOG: Tokyo & Kyoto 2025

## Day 1: Arrival in Shinjuku
Checked into the hotel at 4 PM. The neon lights are even brighter than in the photos.
- Dinner at Omoide Yokocho (Piss Alley). The yakitori was incredible.
- Visited the Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building for the free view.

## Day 2: Harajuku and Shibuya
Walked through Takeshita Street. Way too crowded, but the crepes were worth it.
- **Meiji Jingu**: A peaceful escape from the city noise.
- **Shibuya Crossing**: Crossed it four times just for the video.

## Day 3: Shinkansen to Kyoto
The Nozomi train is fast! Saw Mount Fuji through the window for about 30 seconds.
- Checked into a Ryokan near Gion.
- Had a traditional Kaiseki dinner. 12 courses of art.
